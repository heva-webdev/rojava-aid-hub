# How We Verify

We only list an organization if it meets **all** requirements below.

## Required checks
- Official website (not a copy / not a social-only page)
- Clear donation page on the official site
- Payment method shows the organization name (or legally verified payment processor)
- Recent updates or activity (news, reports, posts, or updates)

## What we do NOT do
- We do not collect donations
- We do not run campaigns
- We do not represent any organization
