# Transparency & Legal

## What this platform is
Rojava Aid Hub is an **information platform** that links to verified organizations.

## What we do NOT do
- We do not collect or process donations
- We do not store payment details
- We do not represent any organization
- We do not run fundraising campaigns

## Donations
All donations are made **directly on official websites** of the listed organizations.

## Disclaimer
Information may change over time. If you notice an outdated link or issue, please contact us.
